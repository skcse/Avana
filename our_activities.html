Our Activities

These are few steps taken by us to fulfill the main motive of this TEAM. We know we are way behind to make India a prosperous country but at least we can do our bit to take the Indian odyssey further ahead, can’t we?

Old Age Home Visit

When a child is born, his parents' eyes are filled with tears of joy. They leave no stone unturned to nurture him with utmost care. What do they expect in return? Some time of his life when they grow old. Unfortunate are those whose children abandon them and leave them to oldage homes when they need them the most. We visited Astha Oldage Home in Indore on Sunday ie. 18th January, 2015 to spend some time with these great parents and spread happiness in their lives.

Teach The Future

Here, every weekend, a group of few volunteers from different batches of B.Tech., M.Tech., M.Sc. and Ph.D. devote approximately 2.5 hours teaching these students Maths and English in a better & innovative way.

Cloth Donation

We believe that just by re-using our old clothes, the problem of clothing for the poor can be solved to a very large extent. We organise cloth donation drive throughout the year where students and other members of our institute come forward to donate their old clothes and help the desperate and needy

Blood Donation
